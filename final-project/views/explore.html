About us

Explore MonoMuse

Mission Statement

MonoMuse is dedicated to exploring the evolving relationship between art, technology, and society. Our mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by showcasing innovative exhibitions that highlight how digital tools, emerging technologies, and human imagination shape the future of artistic expression.

Who We Serve

MonoMuse serves artists, students, researchers, and the general public who are interested in the intersection of art and technology. Our spaces are designed for learning, experimentation, and conversation.

Milestones

  • 2023: Expanded the museum space and launched international artist collaborations.
  • 2018: Opened the Interactive Media Lab for creative coding experiments.
  • 2016: Debuted the museum with its first exhibition on digital storytelling.
