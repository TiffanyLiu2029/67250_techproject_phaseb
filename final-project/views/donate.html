Support MonoMuse

Membership and donations help keep creative access open

MonoMuse depends on community support to fund exhibitions, student workshops, artist talks, and inclusive public programming. This page helps visitors understand how they can give back.

Ways to support

$25 Friend

Helps support free gallery guides and visitor materials.

$75 Studio Circle

Supports artist demonstrations and creative technology workshops.

$150 Member Plus

Supports exhibitions while providing member event invitations and museum updates.

Membership benefits

  • Early access to exhibition previews and special programs
  • Priority updates about new installations and public events
  • Support for student visits, community access, and educational resources

Ready to support?

This class project does not process real payments, but the page fulfills the visitor story by clearly explaining donation and membership options.
