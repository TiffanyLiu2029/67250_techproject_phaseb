Before You Check Out

Have your preferred visit date, ticket quantity, and contact email ready. Student and member tickets require a valid ID at entry.

Visitor Policies

  • Tickets are timed for the selected date.
  • Children under 18 must be accompanied by an adult.
  • Please arrive 10 minutes before your reservation window.
  • Questions can be directed to the front desk on arrival.
